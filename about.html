About Blue Koi Botanicals

Blue Koi Botanicals was founded with the belief that everyday care can be both grounding and beautiful. Each product is thoughtfully formulated in small batches with close attention to ingredients, texture, and ritual.

Woman-owned and locally made in Charlottesville, Virginia, Blue Koi Botanicals values simplicity, transparency, and balance in both formulation and practice.
