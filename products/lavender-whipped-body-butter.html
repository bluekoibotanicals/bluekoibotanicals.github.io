A rich whipped body butter with lavender essential oil, ylang ylang essential oil, honey fragrance oil, and arrowroot powder for a softer, less greasy finish.

Lavender Whipped Body Butter is made for a cushiony moisturizing ritual with a calm, floral scent profile.

Shea butter, cocoa butter, meadowfoam seed oil, jojoba oil, and coconut oil give the formula its rich feel, while arrowroot powder helps soften the finish so it does not feel overly greasy on skin.

How to Use

Massage a small amount onto clean, dry skin.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place.

Benefits

  • Arrowroot powder helps create a smoother, less greasy after-feel.
  • Shea and cocoa butters cushion dry-feeling skin.
  • Meadowfoam and jojoba oils help skin feel soft, supple, and conditioned.
  • Beeswax helps seal in moisture for longer-lasting comfort.

Warning

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

Ingredients

  • Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter
  • Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil
  • Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Seed Butter
  • Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil
  • Maranta Arundinacea Root Powder
  • Cetyl Alcohol
  • Cera Alba (Beeswax)
  • Tocopherol (Vitamin E)
  • Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil (Lavender Essential Oil)
  • Cananga Odorata Flower Oil (Ylang Ylang Essential Oil)
  • Fragrance (Parfum) (Honey Fragrance Oil)
