A warm, rich whipped body butter with cedarwood essential oil, vanilla fragrance oil, lavender, ylang ylang, honey fragrance oil, and arrowroot powder for a smoother finish.

Cedarwood Vanilla Whipped Body Butter is the cozier body butter option, built around a soft woodsy-sweet scent profile.

The formula combines shea butter, cocoa butter, meadowfoam seed oil, jojoba oil, coconut oil, beeswax, vitamin E, and arrowroot powder to leave skin feeling moisturized without an overly greasy finish.

How to Use

Massage a small amount onto clean, dry skin.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place.

Benefits

  • A warm, cozy, high-value body butter for customers who want deep moisture without a plain unscented experience.
  • Arrowroot powder helps tame the oily finish, so the formula feels rich and substantial without sitting on skin like straight butter.
  • Shea and cocoa butters cushion dry-feeling areas, while meadowfoam and jojoba leave a smoother conditioned feel.
  • Cedarwood, vanilla, lavender, ylang ylang, and honey create a woodsy-sweet scent that feels expensive and memorable.

Warning

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

Ingredients

  • Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter
  • Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil
  • Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Seed Butter
  • Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil
  • Maranta Arundinacea Root Powder
  • Cetyl Alcohol
  • Cera Alba (Beeswax)
  • Tocopherol (Vitamin E)
  • Juniperus Virginiana Oil (Cedarwood Essential Oil)
  • Fragrance (Parfum) (Vanilla Fragrance Oil)
  • Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil (Lavender Essential Oil)
  • Cananga Odorata Flower Oil (Ylang Ylang Essential Oil)
  • Fragrance (Parfum) (Honey Fragrance Oil)
