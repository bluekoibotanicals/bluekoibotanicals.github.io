A simple, unscented magnesium chloride body mist made for customers who want the core formula without added fragrance or essential oils.

How to Use

Shake well before use. Spray onto body and massage into skin.

Benefits

  • A clean, no-scent mineral mist for customers who want a simple body-care step without fragrance or essential oils.
