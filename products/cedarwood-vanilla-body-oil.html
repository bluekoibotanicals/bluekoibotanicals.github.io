Cedarwood Vanilla Body Oil

Made with premium meadowfoam seed oil · Net Contents 4 fl oz (118 mL)

A warm, cozy body oil with cedarwood essential oil, vanilla fragrance oil, lavender, ylang ylang, and honey for a rounded woodsy-sweet scent.

Cedarwood Vanilla Body Oil brings a deeper, woodsy profile to the body oil line.

The base combines meadowfoam and jojoba seed oils with vitamin E and a warm scent blend of cedarwood, vanilla, lavender, ylang ylang, and honey.

How to Use

Massage onto clean, damp skin.

Benefits

  • Warm, woodsy, and polished—this is the body oil for customers who want cozy moisture without smelling like a generic vanilla product.
  • Meadowfoam and jojoba leave skin feeling smooth and conditioned while keeping the finish lighter than a heavy cream.
  • The cedarwood, vanilla, lavender, ylang ylang, and honey blend gives the scent more depth and staying power in the ritual.
  • Use it after showering when dry-feeling skin needs quick slip, softness, and a more expensive-feeling finish.

Warning

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

Ingredients

  • Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil
  • Tocopherol (Vitamin E)
  • Juniperus Virginiana Oil (Cedarwood Essential Oil)
  • Fragrance (Parfum) (Vanilla Fragrance Oil)
  • Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil (Lavender Essential Oil)
  • Cananga Odorata Flower Oil (Ylang Ylang Essential Oil)
  • Fragrance (Parfum) (Honey Fragrance Oil)
