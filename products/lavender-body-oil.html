Lavender Body Oil

Made with premium meadowfoam seed oil · Net Contents 4 fl oz (118 mL)

$22

A soft, floral body oil created for relaxed everyday moisture with lavender essential oil, ylang ylang essential oil, and a touch of honey fragrance oil.

This body oil is designed to massage easily onto damp skin after a shower, leaving a polished botanical finish.

Meadowfoam seed oil and jojoba seed oil form the lightweight base, while lavender, ylang ylang, and honey create the signature scent blend.

How to Use

Massage onto clean, damp skin.

Benefits

  • Makes skin look instantly more polished after a shower, with a soft sheen that feels intentional instead of oily.
  • Meadowfoam and jojoba oils glide fast over damp skin, helping arms, legs, and shoulders feel smoother and more supple.
  • The lavender, ylang ylang, and honey blend turns everyday moisturizing into a calming floral ritual people will actually repeat.
  • A little goes a long way, making this an easy upgrade from basic lotion when customers want skin that feels cared for.

Warning

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

Ingredients

  • Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil
  • Tocopherol (Vitamin E)
  • Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil (Lavender Essential Oil)
  • Cananga Odorata Flower Oil (Ylang Ylang Essential Oil)
  • Fragrance (Parfum) (Honey Fragrance Oil)
