A rich unscented whipped body butter with arrowroot powder for a smoother, less greasy finish and no added fragrance or essential oils.

Unscented Whipped Body Butter is made for customers who want the same rich texture without added scent.

The formula uses shea butter, cocoa butter, meadowfoam seed oil, jojoba oil, coconut oil, beeswax, vitamin E, and arrowroot powder for a plush feel that absorbs more elegantly than a traditional heavy butter.

How to Use

Massage a small amount onto clean, dry skin.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place.

Benefits

  • Arrowroot powder helps create a smoother, less greasy feel.
  • No added fragrance or essential oils for customers who prefer a simpler scent profile.
  • Shea and cocoa butters cushion dry-feeling skin.
  • Meadowfoam and jojoba oils help skin feel soft, supple, and conditioned.

Warning

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

Ingredients

  • Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter
  • Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil
  • Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Seed Butter
  • Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil
  • Maranta Arundinacea Root Powder
  • Cetyl Alcohol
  • Cera Alba (Beeswax)
  • Tocopherol (Vitamin E)
