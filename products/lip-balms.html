Botanical Lip Balms

Small, polished, and easy to carry, Blue Koi lip balms are made for soft everyday care with the same botanical style as the full body collection.

The lip balm line brings Blue Koi into the pocket: a fresh eucalyptus spearmint balm for a clean cooling feel, and a honey lavender balm for a softer, soothing botanical scent.

Eucalyptus Spearmint

A clean, crisp balm with eucalyptus and spearmint for a fresh botanical finish.

Honey Lavender

A soft, cozy balm with lavender and honey flavor for a gentle, comforting scent.

How to Use

Apply to lips as needed throughout the day. Store in a cool, dry place and avoid leaving tubes in direct heat.

  • Meadowfoam seed oil
  • Vitamin E oil
  • Essential oils or lip-safe flavor oil by scent

Product Notes

For external use only. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. Essential oils and flavor oils are used lightly for a polished, lip-friendly scent profile.
