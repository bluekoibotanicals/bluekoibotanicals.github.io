A fresh, cooling lip balm with lavender and spearmint in a summer-stable botanical base.

Lavender Spearmint Lip Balm is pocket-sized, polished, and easy to keep nearby for everyday use.

Beeswax, cocoa seed butter, shea butter, meadowfoam seed oil, jojoba seed oil, and vitamin E form the base.

How to Use

Apply to lips as needed.

Benefits

  • A fresh, clean-feeling balm that makes lips feel smooth and conditioned without a waxy, cheap finish.
  • Beeswax and cocoa butter give it structure, while meadowfoam, shea, and jojoba make the glide feel more polished.
  • Lavender and spearmint create a cool, refreshing flavor profile that makes repeat application feel good.
  • A strong impulse-buy product: small, useful, giftable, and easy for customers to add at checkout.

Warning

For external use only. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

Ingredients

  • Cera Alba (Beeswax)
  • Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Seed Butter
  • Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil
  • Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil
  • Tocopherol (Vitamin E)
  • Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil
  • Mentha Spicata (Spearmint) Leaf Oil
