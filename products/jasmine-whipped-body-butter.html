A polished floral whipped body butter with jasmine fragrance oil, ylang ylang essential oil, and arrowroot powder for a rich but less greasy feel.

Jasmine Whipped Body Butter brings an elegant floral scent to the rich body butter line.

The formula blends dense botanical butters with meadowfoam and jojoba oils, then uses arrowroot powder to help reduce the heavy, oily feel that rich butters can leave behind.

How to Use

Massage a small amount onto clean, dry skin.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place.

Benefits

  • Arrowroot powder helps the butter feel smoother and less greasy.
  • Rich butters help comfort dry-feeling areas like elbows, knees, and hands.
  • Meadowfoam and jojoba oils leave skin feeling soft and conditioned.
  • The jasmine-ylang ylang scent creates a refined floral body-care ritual.

Warning

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

Ingredients

  • Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter
  • Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil
  • Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Seed Butter
  • Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil
  • Maranta Arundinacea Root Powder
  • Cetyl Alcohol
  • Cera Alba (Beeswax)
  • Tocopherol (Vitamin E)
  • Fragrance (Parfum) (Jasmine Fragrance Oil)
  • Cananga Odorata Flower Oil (Ylang Ylang Essential Oil)
