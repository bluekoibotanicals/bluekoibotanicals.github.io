A soft, soothing lip balm with lavender essential oil and honey fragrance in a firmer botanical base.

Honey Lavender Lip Balm gives the lip balm line a warmer, sweeter profile while keeping the same compact format.

The base combines beeswax, cocoa seed butter, meadowfoam seed oil, shea butter, jojoba seed oil, vitamin E, lavender oil, and honey fragrance oil.

How to Use

Apply to lips as needed.

Warning

For external use only. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

  • Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil
  • Tocopherol (Vitamin E)
  • Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil
  • Fragrance (Parfum) (Honey Fragrance Oil)
