Lavender Magnesium Spray

Refreshing Botanical Mist · Net Contents 4 fl oz (118 mL)

A refreshing lavender botanical mist made for simple body care with magnesium chloride and lavender oil.

Shake well, spray onto the body, and massage into skin as part of a post-shower or evening routine.

This updated formula includes magnesium chloride, water, polysorbate 20, lavender essential oil, and a broad-spectrum preservative system.

How to Use

Shake well before use. Spray onto body and massage into skin.

Benefits

  • A more elevated mineral mist: the lavender scent makes a quick spray feel like a real evening body-care ritual.
  • Magnesium chloride gives the formula a distinctive mineral feel, while lavender keeps the experience soft and spa-like.
  • Lightweight, fast, and easy to layer under oils or butters when customers want a full-body routine.
  • Strong shelf appeal for customers who want a calming scent, a refreshing skin feel, and a product that feels more interesting than basic spray.

Warning

For external use only. Avoid face, eyes, broken skin, and freshly shaved skin. May tingle. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

Ingredients

  • Magnesium Chloride (Magnesium Flakes)
  • Water (Aqua)
  • Polysorbate 20
  • Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil
  • Propylene Glycol
  • Diazolidinyl Urea
  • Iodopropynyl Butylcarbamate
